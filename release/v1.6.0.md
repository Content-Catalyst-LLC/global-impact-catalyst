# Global Impact Catalyst v1.6.0

## Review, Quality, and Revision Workflow

This release adds governed review and publication operations without changing the v1.1.0 canonical calculation contract.

### Added

- SQLite migration 7
- Workspace-scoped reviewer roles and permissions
- Review assignments, priorities, due dates, and statuses
- Threaded comments with resolution states
- Weighted quality assessments and descriptive grades
- Approval, change-request, rejection, and abstention decisions
- Immutable contract revision snapshots with SHA-256 history
- Material correction records and resulting-revision links
- Publication drafts bound to exact content hashes
- Approval, quality, correction, and stale-content publication gates
- Publication, withdrawal, and event history
- Review-workflow export, schema, restore, service methods, CLI commands, WordPress tables, REST routes, and shortcode

### Compatibility identities

- Canonical contract: 1.1.0
- Evidence repository: 1.3.0
- Indicator registry: 1.4.0
- Measurement repository: 1.5.0
- Review workflow: 1.6.0
- Workspace bundle: 1.6.0
- Database schema: 7

# Global Impact Catalyst v1.5.0

## Observations, Beneficiaries, Budgets, and Outcome Portfolios

v1.5.0 turns the persistent impact repository into an operational program-measurement environment. Initiatives can now retain multi-period observations, aggregate reach, financial records, result relationships, contribution context, and guarded cross-initiative outcome portfolios.

### Highlights

- Five explicit observation data states and revision lineage
- Aggregate-only beneficiary governance and overlap disclosure
- Budgets, expenditures, funding, commitments, and explicit currency conversion
- Transparent cost-per-output, cost-per-outcome, and cost-per-beneficiary metrics
- Output, outcome, and long-term-impact relationship graph
- External-factor and contribution-note records
- Unit-, period-, missing-data-, and overlap-aware portfolio aggregation
- Measurement repository and complete workspace export/restore
- SQLite migration 6, Python/CLI APIs, and authenticated WordPress interface

### Compatibility

The canonical contract and browser/Python calculation engine remain v1.1.0. Evidence formats remain v1.3.0. The indicator registry remains v1.4.0. v1.5.0 is additive and migrates existing v1.4.0 repositories in place.

### Boundary

Beneficiary records are aggregate only. Cost ratios do not prove efficiency or value for money. Contribution records do not establish causality. Portfolio aggregation does not prove attribution or remove double-counting risk beyond its disclosed rules.

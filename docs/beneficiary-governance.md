# Beneficiary Governance — v1.5.0

Global Impact Catalyst uses aggregate beneficiary records. Core workflows are not a case-management or person-record system.

## Definitions

A beneficiary definition records reach type, counting method, privacy level, overlap policy, and notes. Supported reach types are direct, indirect, and combined. Counting can represent unique people, estimated unique people, encounters, households, organizations, or a documented alternative.

## Observations

Beneficiary observations store a period, aggregate count, data state, aggregate dimensions, and optional overlap estimate. They reject likely direct identifiers including names, email addresses, phone numbers, street addresses, dates of birth, government identifiers, and person-level IDs.

## Reach summaries

A beneficiary summary reports direct, indirect, and combined counts, overlap deductions, adjusted reach, data-state disclosures, and the privacy boundary. An adjusted count is only as reliable as the stated counting and overlap assumptions; it is not an independently verified count.

# Connected Public-Interest Impact Intelligence Platform

Global Impact Catalyst v2.0.0 connects the governed v1.x repositories into one institutional platform without changing the canonical v1.1.0 calculation contract.

## Platform records

- institutions, members, roles, and explicit permissions;
- institution-to-workspace relationships and policies;
- verified platform connections and declared capabilities;
- evidence-to-decision pathways represented as governed graphs;
- deterministic orchestration workflows and immutable run records;
- unified snapshots bound to hashes from evidence, registry, measurement, review, analysis, reporting, integration, and production repositories;
- correlated platform events for operational traceability.

## Governance boundaries

Platform roles do not replace WordPress, operating-system, database, or institutional authorization. The platform records intended permissions and enforces them through its application service. It does not certify impact, prove causation, or make institutional decisions automatically.

## Compatibility

v2.0.0 uses database migration 12 and workspace bundle 2.0.0. Earlier repository formats remain embedded at their established versions and are restored in dependency order.
